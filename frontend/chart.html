American Sign Language Chart

This chart shows the American Sign Language alphabet that our application can recognize.

Tips for Better Recognition

  • Make sure your hand is well-lit and clearly visible to the camera
  • Position your hand in the center of the frame
  • Hold each sign steady for a moment to allow for recognition
  • For letters G, Z, and P, exaggerate the hand position slightly
  • Use the "Add to Sentence" button to manually add detected characters
  • Practice with the chart to improve recognition accuracy
